- What user problem does it solve?

- Who is the secondary user, if any?

- What constraints shape the interaction?

- supporting jobs

- business or operational success signal

## Constraints
- technical constraints
- product constraints
- accessibility constraints

## Risks
- confusion risks
- trust risks
- implementation risks

## Non Goals
- what this iteration should not attempt to solve
