# Massive Skill Intelligence Taxonomy

This taxonomy is fixed and deterministic. Group assignment follows:

1. Exact skill-id overrides.
2. Priority-ordered keyword rules.
3. `general-specialists` fallback.

## Groups

### ecosystem-governance
- Purpose: Govern skill lifecycle, catalog integrity, portability, and policy.
- Value: Reliable visibility and low-drift skill operations.

### orchestration-runtime-reliability
- Purpose: Coordinate orchestration, launch/runtime behavior, and reliability guardrails.
- Value: Stable event routing and fewer runtime failures.

### git-repo-delivery
- Purpose: Manage git hygiene, release flows, and repo harmonization.
- Value: Safer collaboration and cleaner delivery.

### security-privacy-threat
- Purpose: Threat modeling, hardening, and privacy-safe engineering.
- Value: Stronger defensive posture and reduced leakage risk.

### diagnostics-telemetry
- Purpose: Investigate failures, anomalies, and telemetry semantics.
- Value: Faster root-cause analysis and better observability.

### ml-model-engineering
- Purpose: Model quality, inference stability, fine-tuning, and hardware optimization.
- Value: Better model performance and reliability.

### qa-code-audit
- Purpose: Testing automation and regression-first quality audits.
- Value: Higher confidence change velocity.

### ux-automation-artifacts
- Purpose: UI/UX workflows, browser automation, screenshots, and PDFs.
- Value: Better interface quality and artifact generation.

### platform-ops-jarvis-openclaw
- Purpose: Jarvis/OpenClaw runtime operations under constrained hardware.
- Value: Reliable operations and guarded upgrades.

### research-strategy-productivity
- Purpose: Research intelligence, planning support, and productivity acceleration.
- Value: Stronger strategic decisions and faster execution.

### general-specialists
- Purpose: Deterministic fallback for skills outside explicit taxonomy rules.
- Value: Complete coverage without uncategorized gaps.
